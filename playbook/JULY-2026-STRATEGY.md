## 4. The commenting system (the dormant lever — biggest near-term unlock)
This is *off* right now. Turning it on is the single highest-ROI move this month.
- **Build the Power List:** 20–30 targets in the app's **Creators tab / Engagement Hub** — ICP founders &
  agency owners + cold-email peers + adjacent RevOps/sales-ops. Columns: category · name · profile · last-engaged.
- **Daily: 5–10 comments** on power-list posts, in the **first 30–60 min** (be in the first 3–5 comments).
- **The 4 comment types:** value-add (share proof/tactic), challenge (respectful pushback/question),
  supportive (validate + your angle), witty. **Never "great post." Never sell in comments.**
- **Reply to every comment on your own posts in the first 30 min** (+30% engagement, doubles comment count).
- **Comment → lead:** capture commenters on your winners → enrich (Clay) → warm DM. Warm beats cold every time.
- **Your engagement habits train the algorithm** on who you are — comment in your ICP's world, not generic feeds.

## 6. DMs (attraction, not pitch-slapping)
Flow: thoughtful comment → they reply → move to DM with **value, not a pitch** ("ask permission to sell").
- **Script A (comment thread → DM):** *"Hey [name], loved your take on [X] — we ran into the same thing
  scaling outbound for a client. Happy to share the exact play we used if useful?"*
- **Script B (lead-magnet delivery):** *"Here's the [magnet] you asked for: [link]. Curious — what's your
  biggest cold-email bottleneck right now?"* (opens a real conversation).
- **Script C (re-engage a profile viewer):** *"Saw you checked out the profile — if you're working on cold
  email, happy to send our [teardown/checklist], no pitch."*
- Book a call **only after** you've given value. Keep it human.
