### Lead Magnet Library — Tribe Digital (likely Matthew Brown, medium confidence)
- Hard-number-in-title naming pattern across nearly all 40 entries, not just a few standouts —
  reads as a deliberate house style: "1 Post = $20,000+", "ISN Launch Breakdown: 45 Sales, $27k
  Revenue, And 12 Lessons", "How I Booked 43 Sales Calls in 60 Days...", "3 SOPs To Save You 100+
  Hours Over The Next Year" — copyable wholesale for our own lead magnet titling
- Counter-narrative proof point as its own standalone magnet: "One client making $1M/year through
  content with 7 average likes per post" — challenges the assumption that engagement metrics
  predict revenue
- Index-page-as-lead-magnet format: the top-level directory page IS the deliverable, no separate
  opt-in gate — just a curated, un-ordered list of 40 titled resources each linking to its own
  Notion sub-page. Worth testing for our own "Lead Magnets (Ours)" collection once it has enough
  entries
- Custom-slug tell: only 2 of 40 entries carry a clean custom slug instead of the Notion default
  (`content-delegation`, `follow-up-framework`) — implies those two are the ones actively promoted
  elsewhere; a signal for which of our own magnets deserve the same slug investment

- Creator attribution had to be inferred (cover-image branding + a self-referential entry +

### Aidan's Lead Magnet Masterclass — Aidan Collins
- Naming: Desired Outcome + Topic + Container Word (Blueprint/Playbook/Kit/Vault/System), 3-5
  words — 'sounds like something you'd pay $50 for'
- 10-point post rubric (curiosity-gap hook, number in opener, 4-6 checkmark deliverables, single
  ALL-CAPS keyword CTA, no emojis, 800-1200 chars) — run against every Bleed AI LM post before
  shipping
- 3-step CTA: Like -> Comment ONE-WORD-KEYWORD -> Connect; single keyword = herd-effect social
  proof
- Deliverable stack: 4-6 checkmark lines, numbers in 2+, parenthetical benefit in 2+
- DM within 2 hours with a 'quick poll' question, NOT a qualifying question — doesn't feel like a
  pitch
- Cadence claim: 2-3 lead magnets per week minimum
