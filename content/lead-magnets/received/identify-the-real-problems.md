# Identify the Real Problems

**Source:** unknown (sub-page inside 'The Ultimate Outbound Sales Playbook') — https://www.notion.so/Identify-the-Real-Problems-21d0435e95c7804aa322cea83b605a7b?pvs=21
**Topic:** offer-creation · **Type:** worksheet/template

## Key takeaways
- Every prospect problem falls into one of 4 buckets: they want a better outcome, they doubt they'll succeed, it feels like too much work, or it takes too long — a reusable diagnostic framework.
- 3-step worksheet flow: (1) List every pain/friction/excuse specifically, (2) flip each into a named solution ('How to build a list of 500 qualified leads in 30 minutes'), (3) brainstorm 'delivery vehicles' — the packaging format for that solution (script+video, swipe file, sheet template, course, database, done-for-you call).
- Explicit example delivery-vehicle lists given per category: cold outreach (script+breakdown video, swipe file, sheet template, course), lead sourcing (pre-qualified database, scraping guide, AI agent, 1:1 call), sales call prep (Glyphic briefing system, checklist, buyer-signal training, live teardown).
- Framing line: 'You're not just solving problems — you're removing roadblocks to speed up their results.'

## Excerpt
What's standing between your prospect and their goal? List out every pain point, friction, excuse, and limiting belief. Pro tip: every problem falls under one of these 4 categories — they want a better outcome, they doubt they'll succeed, it feels like too much work, it takes too long. Turn Problems into Solutions: flip each problem on its head, describing what the fix should look like ('How to write cold emails that actually get responses'). Delivery Vehicles: brainstorm how those solutions could be packaged — for cold outreach: a cold email script + breakdown video, a swipe file of proven subject lines/CTAs, a Google Sheet template with personalization logic, a short course on writing/testing sequences.
