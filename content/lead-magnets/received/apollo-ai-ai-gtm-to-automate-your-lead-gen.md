# Apollo.ai: AI GTM to Automate Your Lead Gen

**Source note:** Growth Exe(cution).


## Key takeaways
- Apollo's AI Assistant across their platform: builds custom prospect lists, runs AI research on companies, creates outreach sequences, and automates workflows via natural-language chat prompts.
- Demo'd workflow: built an ICP-based list from Apollo's 'AI Content Center', ran AI research to qualify companies on 'sales team scaling challenges', auto-moved qualified contacts into a personalized outreach sequence — entirely via prompts.
- Also demo'd inbound-routing automation: checks a website visitor's company size/industry and auto-routes them to either an outreach sequence or a Slack alert, built conversationally.
- Beta access: available on all plans including free (some features cost credits), currently admin-only; influencers get exclusive early access + a 90-day Pro trial with 50,000 credits.
