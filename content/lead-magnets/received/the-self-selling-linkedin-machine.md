# The Self-Selling LinkedIn Machine

**Source note:** Full page content extracted, including the entire tool chain and named case-study results.


## Key takeaways
- Claims taking a new product from $0 to $4,500 MRR in 30 days (May 2025 update: $9,500 MRR within 4 months), driven mostly by direct resource sales, not sales calls.
- 4-phase system: Phase 1 'value-bait' LinkedIn post to test demand before building anything, Phase 2 build the guide only if the post gets traction, Phase 3 repost/retarget with Meta ads to readers who didn't buy, Phase 4 automate lead capture and delivery end-to-end.
- Named tool stack: LeadShark (scrapes commenters who type a keyword like 'AUTO'), NocoDB (lead database), n8n (glue automation), GetSales (auto-DMs the guide + follow-ups), Encharge (email nurture + Facebook Custom Audience sync for retargeting).
- CTA mechanic: ask commenters to type a single keyword instead of using a form/link — claimed to boost algorithmic reach, '100+ qualified leads in 48 hours' on a resonant post.
- Upsell 'Growth Garden' at $600/month; case-study numbers cited: James closed $40K in new contracts, Ivar got 700+ leads from one campaign, Felix Harrer pulled 2,000 leads in under a week using this exact process.
