# Cold Email Frameworks

**Source note:** Litehouse, via Gamma.app.


## Key takeaways
- 4 named frameworks with fill-in-the-blank templates: 'Direct' (outcome+timeframe+mechanism, case study, soft CTA), 'Poke The Bear' (Josh Braun-style provocative question), 'Diagnosis' (personalization -> assessment -> solution -> outcome, credits Eric Nowoslawski), 'Case Study' (similar business + outcome + timeframe + mechanism).
- 'Poke The Bear' verbatim examples: SEO -> "How do you know competitors like {{competitor_name}} aren't employing {{strategy}} to outrank you?"; cyber security -> "What if you could eliminate security issues for good without spending a fortune on {{expensive_competitor_name}}?"
- Psychology stated explicitly: 'People make logical decisions. High risk + Low reward = responses' — case studies reduce risk AND increase reward simultaneously.
- CTA framing rule: use 'you'/'your' more than 'I'/'we'/'us' — literally instructs auditing pronoun ratio.
- Offer at bottom: cold outbound service pitched as 8-15 meetings/month on a pay-per-show basis.
