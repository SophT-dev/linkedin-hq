## Key takeaways
- Profile-optimization checklist: professional/approachable photo, a clear high-quality banner, a non-salesy headline, and a keyword-filled About section.
- 'What actually gets leads' = storytelling, not generic 'value' posts; 5-element story formula: Backstory, Hook, Visuals, Value, CTA.
- Location-hack for targeted visibility: change your LinkedIn location to match your target audience's geography, build a list of 100 micro-creators posting daily in-niche, comment 10+ times daily on their posts, and use the 'Not Interested' filter to prune your feed.
- Names specific creators as engagement targets by niche: Nick Abraham (SMMA space), Nathan Latka (SaaS space) — a concrete example of niche-creator engagement targeting.
