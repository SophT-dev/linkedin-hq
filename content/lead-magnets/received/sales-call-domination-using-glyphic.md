## Excerpt
Sales calls are won or lost before they start. Most people either over-prepare and drown in research, or under-prepare and hope for the best. Glyphic automatically generates a pre-call brief pulling from your CRM, LinkedIn, Crunchbase, and past call transcripts — no setup needed. It flags objections raised in past calls so you can address them before they resurface, auto-drafts your follow-up email the moment the call ends (send within 5 minutes), logs everything to your CRM automatically, and gives AI coaching on your talk patterns after each call. Conclusion: most businesses don't fail because of bad products — they fail because their systems are broken.
