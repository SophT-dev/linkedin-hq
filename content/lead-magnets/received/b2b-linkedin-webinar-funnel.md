## Key takeaways
- Case-study claim: one client webinar generated $10,000 for the client.
- Practice method for introverted hosts: run a LinkedIn 'audio event' podcast-style with a niche peer, ending the last 15 minutes on audience Q&A, then scrape attendees for a post-event lead-magnet + offer follow-up.

## Excerpt
No one talks about how powerful a LinkedIn webinar can be. We did this webinar for our client that ended up making them $10,000. Step 1: Practice & Warm-Up — find a peer in your niche, invite them to an audio event in podcast style, keep the last 15 minutes for audience Q&A, then scrape and follow up with attendees using a lead magnet + offer. Step 2: Launch — use the 50/50 Rule (half presentation, half live demo), ask for feedback after each session. Pro Tips: 90% of people get better after 3-5 webinars; do one per month; promote via invites and Facebook groups; use the Trojan horse pitch formula.
