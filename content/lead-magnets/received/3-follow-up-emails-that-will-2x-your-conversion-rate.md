## Excerpt
Sending multiple follow-ups isn't just about 'checking in' — it's your chance to reframe your value and address pain points from different angles. Email #1: Personalized Intro + Problem Recognition + Solution (Value Prop) + risk-free Offer. Email #2 (The Reframe): show your solution from a different angle. Email #3 (The Better Offer): if ignored twice, increase trial length or add a bonus — e.g., 'try our platform free for 90 days.' Email #4 (The Other Contact Nudge): politely ask if there's someone else who handles this. A strategic follow-up sequence massively boosts open and reply rates — often by 200% or more.
