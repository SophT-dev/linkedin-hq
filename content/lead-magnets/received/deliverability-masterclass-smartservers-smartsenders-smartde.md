# Deliverability Masterclass: SmartServers, SmartSenders & SmartDelivery

**Source:** SmartLead — https://www.notion.so/Deliverability-Masterclass-SmartServers-SmartSenders-SmartDelivery-18815203fef48143b15ee64844df7a67?pvs=21
**Topic:** tools/tech-stack · **Type:** notion-page

## Key takeaways
- SmartServers = dedicated sending infrastructure (avoids shared-server reputation contamination), priced at $39/server/month.
- SmartSenders = bulk-purchased, ready-to-use inbox+domain bundles with pricing given: Inbox Automate (Google) $15/domain + $4/mailbox; InboxInfra (Outlook) $16/domain + $5/mailbox; Mailreef (SMTP) $285 one-time for 15 domains / $299 for 75 mailboxes.
- SmartDelivery / 'Smart Inbox' = a placement-testing feature: sends test emails to real ESP accounts globally to report inbox vs. promotions vs. spam landing per mailbox + per copy, replacing guesswork from open-rate tracking alone.
- Non-SmartLead users can still use SmartDelivery externally via a 'Seed List' + unique Test ID system.

## Excerpt
Without ensuring emails land in your leads' inboxes, even the most compelling campaigns fail. SmartServers gives dedicated sending infrastructure ($39/server/month) with IP rotation and bounce protection. SmartSenders lets you purchase ready-to-use inboxes/domains in bulk (Inbox Automate: $15/domain + $4/mailbox; InboxInfra: $16/domain + $5/mailbox; Mailreef: $285 one-time for 15 domains). SmartDelivery's 'Smart Inbox' runs a placement test before you go live — sending to test accounts across ESPs globally, then reporting inbox/promotions/spam placement per mailbox and scoring your sequence copy against spam filters, removing reliance on unreliable open-rate tracking.
