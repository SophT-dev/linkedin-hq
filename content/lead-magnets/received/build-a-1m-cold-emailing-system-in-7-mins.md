## Key takeaways
- 6-step cold-email system: (1) nail a specific ICP, (2) source/filter leads via Google Maps/Apollo/Crunchbase/Clay with 'waterfalling' (only enrich leads that pass key filters, to save credits), (3) write short 2-4 sentence personalized copy with a tangible offer (free audit/trial/data insight), (4) automate personalization at scale (pull a recent LinkedIn post or case study, always cite the source e.g. 'SimilarWeb'), (5) send/track via Instantly or SmartLead with a named 3-email sequence structure, (6) follow-up variations: Reframe (new benefit), Better Offer (longer trial/bonus), Redirect ('who's the best contact for this?').
- Pro tip: re-check the same list a few months later with fresh copy/filters/triggers (e.g. 'recently joined', 'new funding') since prospect needs change fast.

## Excerpt
A well-structured cold email system can be the difference between booking zero calls and consistently hitting your sales goals. Nail your ICP, find & filter quality leads via Clay waterfalling, write short 2-4 sentence copy with a strong tangible offer, automate personalization selectively (pull one AI-sourced detail, keep the core message consistent, always cite the source), and send with a 3-email sequence: First Email = intro + problem + unique offer; Second Email = a different angle; Third Email = lower friction / easy next step. Follow-up variations: Reframe (new benefit), Better Offer (longer trial or bonus), Redirect ('who's the best contact at your company for this?'). Re-check your list in a few months with fresh copy and updated triggers.
