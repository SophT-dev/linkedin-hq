# Signal Map

<!-- This module defines the buying signals you monitor and how to act on them. Paste it into the Signal Map section of any template. Signal-driven outbound consistently outperforms batch-and-blast because you're reaching people at the moment they're most likely to care. -->

## Signal Categories

### Hiring Signals (P0 - Highest Intent)

| Signal | What to look for | Where to find it | Action |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|--------|
| Hiring for your buyer persona | [e.g., Job posting for VP Sales] | LinkedIn Jobs, Indeed, company careers page | Immediate outreach - they're building the function you sell into |
| Hiring for the role your product replaces | [e.g., Posting for manual data entry roles] | Job boards | Outreach with ROI angle - show how your product eliminates the need |
| Rapid team expansion | [e.g., 5+ open roles in target department] | LinkedIn, BuiltWith hiring data | Signal of budget and urgency - prioritize |

### Funding Signals (P0)

| Signal | What to look for | Where to find it | Action |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|--------|
| Recent fundraise | [e.g., Series B announcement in last 90 days] | Crunchbase, TechCrunch, PR newswires | Outreach within 2 weeks - budget is allocated, they're spending |
| IPO / acquisition activity | [e.g., IPO filing, acquisition announcement] | SEC filings, news | Reach out with scale/compliance angle |

### Tech Adoption Signals (P1)

| Signal | What to look for | Where to find it | Action |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|--------|
| Adopted complementary tool | [e.g., Just started using HubSpot] | BuiltWith, G2 reviews, job postings mentioning tools | Position as natural next step in their stack |
| Migrating platforms | [e.g., Moving from Salesforce to HubSpot] | Job postings, G2, LinkedIn posts | Outreach with migration support angle |
| Removed competitor | [e.g., Dropped competitor product from tech stack] | BuiltWith changes, G2 | P0 - Immediate outreach, they're actively evaluating alternatives |

### Content & Social Signals (P1)

| Signal | What to look for | Where to find it | Action |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|--------|
| Prospect posted about your problem space | [e.g., LinkedIn post about outbound challenges] | LinkedIn, Twitter | Engage on post, then DM with relevant resource |
| Company published relevant blog | [e.g., Blog about scaling sales team] | Company blog, RSS feeds | Reference the blog in your outreach |
| Speaking at relevant event | [e.g., Presenting at SaaStr] | Event agendas, LinkedIn | Attend/reference the talk in outreach |

### Intent Signals (P1)

| Signal | What to look for | Where to find it | Action |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|--------|
| Visited your website | [e.g., Multiple page views on pricing page] | Web analytics, Clearbit Reveal | Fast follow-up - they're actively evaluating |
| Searched for your category | [e.g., G2 category page views] | Bombora, G2 Buyer Intent | Outreach with comparison/proof points |
| Downloaded competitor content | [e.g., Competitor whitepaper download] | Intent data providers | Position differentiation angle |
| Researched your category via AI | [e.g., Prospect used Perplexity or similar to research your product category] | Perplexity activity (if available), search intent data | Outreach referencing the problem they're researching |

### Competitive Displacement Signals (P2)

| Signal | What to look for | Where to find it | Action |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|--------|
| Negative competitor review | [e.g., 1-star G2 review for competitor] | G2, Capterra, TrustRadius | Outreach with switching offer |
| Competitor contract renewal window | [e.g., Known renewal dates] | CRM notes, industry intel | Reach out 60-90 days before renewal |

### Timing Signals (P2)

| Signal | What to look for | Where to find it | Action |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|--------|
| Fiscal year start | [e.g., New budget cycle in January] | Public filings, industry norms | Outreach 1-2 months before fiscal year |
| Leadership change | [e.g., New CRO or VP Sales hired] | LinkedIn, press releases | New leaders buy new tools - reach out within 30 days |
| Company milestone | [e.g., Hit 100 employees, opened new office] | LinkedIn, Crunchbase | Congratulate + position your solution for their new scale |

## Filled-In Examples

### Example 1: Sales Engagement Platform

**P0 signals monitored:**
- Hiring SDR Manager or VP Sales (LinkedIn Jobs, daily)
- Series A–C funding in last 60 days (Crunchbase alerts)
- Removed Outreach.io or SalesLoft from tech stack (BuiltWith, weekly)

**P1 signals monitored:**
- Adopted HubSpot or Salesforce (BuiltWith, weekly)
- Prospect posted about "outbound is broken" on LinkedIn (Sales Navigator saved search)

### Example 2: Data Infrastructure Tool

**P0 signals monitored:**
- Hiring Data Engineers or Analytics Engineers (LinkedIn Jobs, daily)
- Series B+ funding in last 90 days (Crunchbase alerts)

**P1 signals monitored:**
- Adopted Snowflake or Databricks (BuiltWith, weekly)
- Published blog post about data pipeline challenges (RSS, weekly)
