## How to use these prompts

1. Open the file for the asset type you want to create.
2. Copy the prompt under the "Prompt" heading.
3. Fill in the bracketed placeholders with your specific information.
4. Paste the prompt into a Claude session.
5. If the suggested pairing hook is active in your `~/.claude/settings.json`, the output will be automatically saved or queued when Claude finishes.
