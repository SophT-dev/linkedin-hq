# Investor Pitch Deck — 10 Slides

## Purpose

Generate a complete 10-slide investor pitch deck outline with full slide content. Use this when preparing for a fundraising conversation, accelerator application, or any meeting where you need to communicate company potential to a capital allocator.

## Prompt

```
You are a pitch deck writer with experience reviewing hundreds of Series A and seed decks. Write a complete 10-slide investor pitch deck for the following company.

Company name: [COMPANY NAME]
One-sentence description: [WHAT THE COMPANY DOES AND FOR WHOM]
Problem being solved: [THE SPECIFIC PAIN POINT, WITH QUANTIFIED STAKES IF POSSIBLE]
Solution: [HOW THE PRODUCT SOLVES IT]
Business model: [HOW MONEY IS MADE — SUBSCRIPTION, USAGE, SERVICES, ETC.]
Traction: [KEY METRICS — REVENUE, USERS, GROWTH RATE, NOTABLE CUSTOMERS]
Market size: [TAM, SAM, SOM IF KNOWN — OR DESCRIBE THE MARKET]
Team: [FOUNDERS AND KEY HIRES, ONE SENTENCE EACH]
Ask: [AMOUNT RAISING AND PRIMARY USE OF FUNDS]
Stage: [PRE-SEED / SEED / SERIES A]

For each slide, write:
- Slide title
- The single most important thing this slide must communicate (one sentence)
- All body content: bullet points, sub-bullets, or short paragraphs as appropriate
- A speaker note (2-3 sentences) explaining what to say aloud and what objection to pre-empt

Slides to include:
1. Cover — company name, tagline, contact
2. Problem — the pain, who feels it, what it costs them today
3. Solution — the product, how it works, why now
4. Product — key features or workflow, one concrete use case
5. Market size — TAM/SAM/SOM with source or methodology
6. Business model — how revenue works, unit economics if available
7. Traction — growth chart description, key milestones, social proof
8. Competition — landscape map or comparison table, why you win
9. Team — founders and advisors, why this team for this problem
10. The Ask — amount, use of funds, 18-month milestones

Do not use filler phrases like "revolutionary" or "game-changing." Every claim must be specific. Where data is missing, write "[INSERT METRIC]" as a clear placeholder.
```

## Example Output Description

A structured document with 10 clearly labeled sections. Each section contains a headline, body copy in bullet form, and a speaker note. The cover slide includes a punchy tagline and contact line. The problem slide quantifies the cost of inaction. The traction slide describes a growth chart with labeled axes and key milestone callouts. The ask slide specifies a dollar amount, three use-of-funds line items with percentages, and three 18-month milestones.
