## Excerpt
The Pattern-Interrupt Principles: avoid 'Thanks for connecting' (76% fewer responses), use strategic imperfection/casual formatting, keep messages under 50 words (response drops 10% per 25 words over), lead with relevance not your offering, focus on one thing only (43% higher response). Template #2 - The Intentionally Casual Approach (65% Response Rate): 'hi [first name] — noticed you're dealing with [specific challenge] at [company]. we've built a solution that helps [specific role] achieve [specific result] without [common pain point]. what's your biggest challenge with [relevant process] currently?' Intentionally using lowercase and casual language made this the highest-converting template. Template #4 - The Appreciation Opener (66% Response Rate) achieved the highest acceptance rate by giving genuine appreciation without asking for anything.
