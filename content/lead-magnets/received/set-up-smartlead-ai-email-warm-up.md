# Set Up Smartlead AI Email Warm-up

**Source:** SmartLead — https://www.notion.so/Set-Up-Smartlead-AI-Email-Warm-up-18815203fef481899db2ea3a28b5ee0b?pvs=21
**Topic:** tools/tech-stack · **Type:** notion-page

## Key takeaways
- 4-step setup: create SmartLead account, connect inbox with correct SPF/DKIM/DMARC, configure AI warmup starting at ~10 emails/day ramping gradually toward a 30% reply-rate benchmark, then monitor/adjust volume if spam issues appear.
- Hard rule stated: give warmup at least two weeks — 'rushing this step is a surefire way to kill your deliverability before you've even started.'

## Excerpt
A proper email warmup is the number one difference-maker between landing in the primary inbox and getting buried in spam. Why it matters: establishes trust (sudden sending spikes get flagged), improves deliverability, and boosts response rates once in the primary inbox. Setup: create a SmartLead account, connect Gmail/Outlook/SMTP with correct DNS, configure AI warmup starting low (~10/day) and ramp gradually aiming for a 30% reply-rate benchmark, then monitor and adjust for spam issues. Key point: give it at least two weeks.
