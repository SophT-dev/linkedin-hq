# Secrets To 64% Reply Rates In 2025

**Source:** SmartLead (references its own Slack community and 'Max' by name) — https://www.notion.so/Secrets-To-64-Reply-Rates-In-2025-18815203fef4816c98c6c6b30dc2924f?pvs=21
**Topic:** cold-email · **Type:** notion-page

## Key takeaways
- Infrastructure mix formula: 50% Google, 40-45% Microsoft, 10% 'experimental' SMTP providers for resiliency; start at 15-25 emails/day per account, never blast 100+/day until copy is validated; use stealth/multi-login browsers + unique payment methods per tenant to avoid detection; warm up 14+ days.
- Data quality: don't rely 100% on Apollo/LinkedIn (over-scraped) — supplement with Yelp/Yellow Pages/Google Maps, industry association member lists, and Northdata.com for EU company data; validate emails with NeverBounce or built-in Clay/SmartLead validators.
- Copy structure (named 4-part): Personalized Intro -> Problem Recognition -> Value Proposition (2-3 sentences) -> low-risk Offer; subject lines should be 1-2 words max (e.g. 'Quick Q').
- Follow-up rule: 1-2 max, then recycle the list after a few months rather than continuing to hammer non-responders.
- Q&A nugget: average enterprise C-level hire allocates budget within 90 days of joining — a trigger worth targeting.
