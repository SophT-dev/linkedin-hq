## Key takeaways
- A 22-template fill-in-the-blank library for LinkedIn lead-magnet posts, each with the same 4-part skeleton: Hook, Content Structure (overview/features/results), Call-to-Action (like+comment keyword+connect), and Hashtags.
- Covers 22 distinct lead-magnet categories: LinkedIn Growth Guide, Lead Magnet System, Sales Strategies/Email Copy, AI/Tools Library, Swipe Files, Ad Strategy/Funnel Breakdown, SOPs/Playbooks, Checklist/Cheatsheet, Campaign Strategy, Educational Video Training, and more.
- Every template uses the same delivery mechanic: 'Like + comment [keyword] + ensure you're connected' to receive the resource via DM — a repeatable engagement-bait pattern usable directly for content ideas.

## Excerpt
Massive inspiration to create winning lead magnets that help establish you as a thought leader while driving interest. Example (Template 1 — LinkedIn Growth Guide): Hook: 'I've spent countless hours perfecting [platform/strategy]—now, I'm sharing my blueprint for FREE!' Content Structure: intro + results/features (follower growth, impressions, leads generated) + a key transformation. CTA: (1) engage with the post, (2) comment a keyword (e.g. 'VALUE'), (3) connect with the poster. Every one of the 22 templates repeats this Hook / Content Structure / CTA / Hashtags skeleton across different content categories.
