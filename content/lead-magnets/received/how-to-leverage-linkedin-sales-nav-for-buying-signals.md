## Excerpt
LinkedIn Sales Navigator is a goldmine for identifying buying signals. A Company Raised Money: Account Tab > Spotlight > Funding Events in Past 12 Months. A Company Uses Specific Technology: Account Tab > Company Attributes > Technologies Used. Rapid Headcount Growth: Account Tab > Spotlight > Department Growth. Companies Hiring for New Positions: Account Tab > Job Opportunities > Hiring on LinkedIn. The average C-Level hire spends $1M on new solutions within their first 90 days — target CXOs via the Spotlight filter plus a recent-job-change filter. Boolean Search: quotes for exact matches, OR to broaden, NOT to exclude, and combine terms and modifiers for precision. Export lead lists to CSV with Evaboot.
