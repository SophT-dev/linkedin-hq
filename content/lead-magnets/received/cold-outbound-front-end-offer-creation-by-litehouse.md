# Cold Outbound Front-End Offer Creation by Litehouse

**Source note:** Litehouse, hosted via Mainspace Media's Notion.


## Key takeaways
- Defines 'front-end offer' as a free/low-cost foot-in-the-door with a 10-point criteria checklist (ideal-client-only, low commitment, fast value within 1-2 weeks, <=30 min of prospect time, free or near-free, solves a painful problem, matches awareness level, clear result, legitimate reason for a call, links to core offer).
- Explicitly calls out Hormozi-style '$100M offers' guarantees as overused/no longer working due to market saturation.
- Names audits as the most overused and weakest front-end offer type — requires prospect effort and doesn't solve anything itself.
- 5-step build process: (1) map every micro-problem your full service solves, (2) choose a templated/systemized solution, (3) design against the 10-point criteria, (4) build the sales process/SOP, (5) package into one sentence like 'We'll help you {outcome} for free.'
- 10 concrete industry examples with exact deliverables: Recruiting Agency -> 'Free Candidate Shortlist: 3 pre-vetted resumes'; Tax Services -> 'Free 20-Minute Tax Savings Audit: 2-3 overlooked deductions'; Web Design -> '1 Free Landing Page Design: Figma file + CRO notes.'

## Excerpt
A front-end offer is exactly what it sounds like... Ever since Hormozi launched $100m offers... the marketplace has been inundated with offers like 'if we don't sign you 5 clients in 30 days we'll work for free'. They worked great, but now they don't... Free Candidate Shortlist: 'We'll send you 3 pre-vetted resumes for your next [role].'
