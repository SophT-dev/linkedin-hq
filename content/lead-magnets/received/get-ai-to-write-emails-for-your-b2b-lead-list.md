# Get AI to Write Emails for Your B2B Lead List

**Source note:** Lasse Flagstad, COO @ Buro Ventures. Contains two full reusable AI prompts.


## Key takeaways
- Author (Lasse Flagstad, COO @ Buro Ventures) claims he used these prompts to book 34 meetings in 6 days.
- 3-step system: Step 1 audience analysis, Step 2 'OfferScan-GPT' prompt that reverse-engineers a competitor's offer into a structured Offer Snapshot grounded in Hormozi's Value Equation, Step 3 'PromptMaestro-GPT' prompt that writes one single-idea, 150-300 word value-first warm email using only that offer context.
- Value Equation reproduced verbatim: raise Dream Outcome + Perceived Likelihood, lower Time Delay + Effort/Sacrifices; naming tactic ('30-Day Sales Jumpstart' vs 'a coaching program') to anchor certainty.
- Email doctrine: one idea per email only, plain conversational tone, short punchy sentences, subject-line branding (e.g. 'Mozi Money Minute') to train recipients to look for you, humor/PS jokes as a memorability lever.
- Explicit self-check gate before output is accepted: teaches one actionable tactic, avoids referencing the offer/case studies directly, stays 150-300 words, ends with a natural CTA.
