# LinkedIn DM Vault 2.0

**Source note:** SalesRobot. Numbers cross-verified against the '13 High-Response Templates' entry's clearer table.


## Key takeaways
- Framed as distilled insights from 'analyzing over 100 high-performing LinkedIn outreach campaigns.'
- Catalogs 18 named DM templates tagged with industry vertical, use-case, delivery type (Message/Connection Request/Voicenote), and a reply-rate number: e.g. 'Saw Your Job Posting' (Connection Request) 45%, 'Ask For Feedback' (Message) 47%, '50 Word' (Message, Tech) 46%, 'Do the Math' (value-based) 37%, 'Compliment' (relationship-building) 48%.
- Organized by use-case category: B2B Sales & Lead Gen, Content Marketing & Media, Professional Services, Recruiting & HR, Networking, Tech.
- Highest reply-rate templates are all non-pitchy, low-friction openers ('Compliment' 48%, 'Ask For Feedback' 47%, '50 Word' 46%) rather than direct sales asks.
